What is PlayHub Mobile?

PlayHub Mobile is a mobile play trailer created to bring movement, imagination, and real play back into children’s everyday spaces.

We partner with preschools and communities to create joyful, screen-free play experiences that encourage movement, connection, and imagination.

For children from crawling age through 1st grade

Designed for daycares, preschools, early elementary programs, camps, and parent-child classes.
